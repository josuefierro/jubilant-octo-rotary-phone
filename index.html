A list of names in Nahuatl

And their english translations

  • Itzcuin-Runs = like a dog
  • Matlalihhuitl = purple feather
  • Omacatl = double gendered god of creation
  • Moyolehuani = the enamored one
  • Nezzahualpilli = a fasting prince
  • Tizocicatzin = ancient king
  • Xochiatlapal = flower wing
  • Malinalxochitl = grass flower
  • Aztlacapaltontli = small wint of bird
  • Ipalnemohuani = he through whom one lives
